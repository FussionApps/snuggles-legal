What Snuggles is

Snuggles is a pet journaling app for owners who want more than a camera roll. Build a timeline for each of your pets, share it with your family, and let the app help you remember the things you'd otherwise lose track of.

A timeline per pet

Photos, videos, captions, notes, and reminders — organized by pet and date, not buried in your phone.

Shared family journals

Invite partners, co-parents, and pet sitters. Everyone sees the same timeline; everyone can add to it.

Smart pet tagging

When you add a photo, Snuggles suggests which of your pets it is — matching is computed on your device.

Milestones & reminders

Birthdays, vet appointments, gotcha-days, and the small recurring moments worth remembering.
